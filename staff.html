Our Staff of Experts

Staff members at Collision Analysis Group LLC have been reconstructing/investigating fatal and sever injury traffic accidents for over 15 years. We have received our core training from Northwestern University Center for Public Safety (NUCPS) and continue training by attending specialty courses and conferences related to the accident reconstruction field (See Curriculum Vitae). Staff members have over 20 years of Law Enforcement experience specializing in certain fields as major crash investigations, criminal investigations, traffic division, evidence technician, and police supervision. Staff members have been utilized by Law Enforcement Agencies to document large outdoor homicide scenes to assist in preparing the case for prosecution. We have also reconstructed/investigated serious accidents for Law Enforcement agencies in the Chicago metropolitan area. Our staff has also conducted Accident Investigation training for Law Enforcement Agencies in Illinois.

Staff members at Collision Analysis Group LLC are Certified Accident Reconstructionist/Specialist with The Accredited Commission for Accident Reconstruction (ACTAR). Staff members are also certified with the Illinois Police Standard and Training Board. As mentioned above, staff has received their core training from Northwestern University Center for Public Safety and continues their training by attending specialty courses and conferences.
