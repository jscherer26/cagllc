Rate Schedule

An invoice will be sent to the client upon completion of the case. Payment is expected within 30 days of receipt of the invoice.

RATES:

Hourly rate is $140.00 for all services with the exception of CDR.

Local Travel to scene/site from Barrington Illinois is billed at $80.00 per hour.

Crash Data retrieval fee is a flat $405.00 which includes copy of retrieved information, written report and photographs (see note)

*note if CDR info is not available due to power outage/interruption during event recording the fee of $405.00 still applies.

*mileage to the site will be billed at .50 per mile; no hourly travel rate of $80.00 applies for CDR service.

Non Local Travel to scene/site from Barrington Illinois is billed $80.00 per hour. All travel expenses will be billed at actual cost (Airfare, Hotel, Rental car, Meals).

Police Agencies contact us for a discounted rate.
