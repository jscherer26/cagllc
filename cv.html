Curriculum Vitae

Kirk Ashleman is the director of Collision Analysis Group LLC. Mr. Ashleman has over twenty years of Law Enforcement experience and is currently employed as a police supervisor in the Chicago metropolitan area. Most of his Law Enforcement career has been spent in specialized units such as Major Accident Investigations, Criminal Investigations and as a Forensic Evidence Technician. His specialty and passion was in Major Accident Investigations. Mr. Ashleman has investigated and reviewed hundreds of crashes ranging from fatalities to low-impact. His skills as a Reconstructionist have been utilized by Law Enforcement agencies in the Chicago metropolitan area. His skills to document large scenes have also been utilized by a homicide task force to prepare cases for prosecution.

His core training was completed in 2001 at the Northwestern University Center for Public Safety (NUCPS). Mr. Ashleman obtained certification as a Reconstruction Specialist in 2001 by the Illinois Police Standard and Training Board. He then pursued certification with The Accredited Commission for Traffic Accident Reconstructionist (ACTAR) in 2005.
