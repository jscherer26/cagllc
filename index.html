Mission Statement

Collision Analysis Group LLC is dedicated to providing professional traffic accident reconstruction/investigation services in an impartial, honest and ethical manner. Our goal is to provide our clientele with clear, concise answers to their particular case in a timely matter.

Our opinions will be based on education, training, principles and years of practical experience in the field. Staff members at Collision Analysis Group LLC are certified Accident Reconstructionist/Specialist with ACTAR (Accredited Commission for Traffic Accident Reconstruction) and the Illinois Police Standards and Training Board.

Our clients consist of professionals in various fields as Personal Injury Attorneys, County Prosecutors, Criminal Defense Attorneys, Insurance Agencies (SIU), and Municipalities. Collision Analysis Group LLC serves the State of Illinois and the Midwest region.
